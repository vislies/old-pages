Welcome to Vis Lies 2015!

Come laugh at classic lies and learn lessons to save your next visualization, or maybe even your life! Vis Lies is being held at the IEEE SciVis conference.

Thursday, October 29
6:15pm – 8:00 pm
Salon 3

Everyone is welcome to come join the fun. You can sit and laugh along with the rest of us, but to double up on the fun grab your own favorite awful visualization and share it. Presenting at Vis Lies is easy. Just bring one or more examples of bad visualization, show it on the projector, and tell us what is wrong. We encourage anyone with a Vis Lie to come on up to the stage, but register in advance to reserve a spot and stake a claim to your choice target before anyone has a chance.
